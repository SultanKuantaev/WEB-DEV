Welcome to Almaty

Discover the heart of Kazakhstan's culture, nature, and history.

Why Visit Almaty?

Almaty, the largest city in Kazakhstan, is a vibrant metropolis surrounded by the majestic Tien Shan mountains. It offers an incredible mix of natural beauty, rich history, and modern attractions. From bustling markets to tranquil hiking trails, there's something for everyone in this beautiful city.

Top Attractions

  • Medeu Ice Skating Rink: The world's highest-altitude skating rink, surrounded by stunning mountain views.
  • Big Almaty Lake: A pristine turquoise lake nestled in the mountains.
  • Kok-Tobe Hill: Take a cable car to the top for panoramic views of the city and a fun-filled amusement park.
  • Green Bazaar: A bustling market where you can sample local food and buy unique souvenirs.

Things to Do

  1. Hike the trails in the Ile-Alatau National Park.
  2. Take a day trip to Charyn Canyon, known as the "Grand Canyon of Kazakhstan."
  3. Experience Kazakh culture through traditional music and dance performances.
  4. Enjoy local delicacies like beshbarmak and baursaks at a cozy restaurant.

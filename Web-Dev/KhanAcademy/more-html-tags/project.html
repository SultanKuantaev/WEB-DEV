Spaghetti Carbonara

  • Time: 30 minutes
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
Spaghetti 400g
Pancetta 150g
Eggs 3 large
Parmesan Cheese 50g
Salt and Pepper To taste

Step 1: Cook the spaghetti in a large pot of salted boiling water until al dente. Reserve 1 cup of pasta water and drain the rest.

Step 2: While the pasta cooks, saut√© pancetta in a pan until crispy.

Step 3: Beat the eggs in a bowl, then mix in the grated Parmesan cheese.

Step 4: Combine the pasta, pancetta, and egg mixture in the pan. Toss quickly, adding pasta water as needed to create a creamy sauce.

Step 5: Season with salt and pepper to taste, then serve immediately.
